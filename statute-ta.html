Legislative Heritage

The North Western Provincial Council has created a strong legal framework for provincial governance by enacting 50 statutes since its inception. These statutes cover various sectors including finance, administration, development, environment, agriculture, and social welfare.

From the 1989 Basic Emergency Fund Statute to the 2018 Provincial Road Passenger Transport Services Statute, each piece of legislation reflects the evolving needs and priorities of the province. These statutes form the backbone of provincial administration and serve as the legal foundation for various development initiatives and governance structures.
